About Siberia Refinery

Leading the energy sector in Kazakhstan with innovation and sustainability

Excellence in Petroleum Refining Since 1999

Siberia Refinery Kazakhstan is a state-of-the-art oil refining facility located in the heart of Kazakhstan's energy corridor. We specialize in producing high-quality petroleum products that meet international standards including ASTM, GOST, and ISO certifications.
